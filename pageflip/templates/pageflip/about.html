About Us

What is PageFlip?

PageFlip is an online home for all your reading needs Whether you like Sci-fi, Romance, Young Adult, and more! You'll find a place here at PageFlip. This place was built by bookworms, for bookworms, and we couldn't be prouder of what we accomplished

How does it work?

Each month our users can enjoy 100* new books, with a special theme each month. Don't worry if you don't fancy a specific genre! We guarantee that each month you'll find a neat sub-genre that fits your mood, and you might even find a new favourite! Your opinions are the most important building block in the PageFlip site, so don't be shy and give our books a rating.

Where can I start?

We're glad you are interested! To start, head over to our registration page and make an account. Yep, it's that easy! From there you can start reading, rating and making new discoveries each month with us

I have more questions

No worries, if you have any concerns or simply want a book recommendation or two, send us an email at pageflipsupport@pageflip.com and we'll get back to you right away

So, what are you waiting for? Start reading! :)
